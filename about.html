About/Whoami

Hi, I'm Deniz Sulmaz
I am currently working in frontend development. But apart from my software interests, I am also closely interested in design, music and sports. I am also an entrepreneur. Although I am currently working in the frontend department in the software field, I am thinking of working in other areas on a long-term basis and I am developing myself in this direction. I have a long way to go in the field of design, instead of staying only in the field of photography/painting, I will also turn to the video editing part of the design. In the field of sports, I am currently interested in bodybuilding and dance. In the field of music, I am working on music production, which I intended to do professionally in the beginning, but currently only for hobby purposes. Even though I already have a busy life, I think this is what makes me active and brings out my creativity.

Vision

I am happy to acquire new information at every moment of my life, to constantly improve myself and to spread it around.
When I come to a certain point in my career, I aim to develop projects in the name of "equality and justice" and to ensure that nobody, especially children, experiences inequality of opportunity in education.
My first goal in my career journey is to break the taboo that a person can only work in one field. Later, I will be trying to break down stereotypes in every field I work in. See you soon with my new projects in the light of Science - Art - Sports
